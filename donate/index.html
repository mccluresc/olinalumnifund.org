By Check

While slightly slower, you can minimize fees by mailing a check directly to the college.

  1. Make the check payable to Olin College of Engineering.
  2. Make the memo Alumni Merit Scholarship Fund.
  3. Address the envelope to: 
    Development Office
Olin College of Engineering
1000 Olin Way, Edison House
Needham, MA 02492-1200
  4. Mail it.
